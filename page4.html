Ranger Buddies - Be a hero everyday

Looking for something fun to do with your kids?

Sign up for Ranger Buddies programme where there will be activities for your child to go on everyday.

Becoming a Ranger Buddy teaches your child how small actions can make a big difference. Through missions everyday, your child will build strong and resilient character traits that are anchored in essential life skills.

With fun missions created at Singapore Zoo, home and locations all around the island, there is always something to discover and for children to continually learn to commit micro-actions.

What's more, receiver Ranger Buddies reward pins and badges they complete their mission.

Go a step further and help us care for our Animal Buddies and their friends through this symbolic adoption program. Get an exclusive Animal Buddy Champion adoption kit when you do!

Registration is free!
